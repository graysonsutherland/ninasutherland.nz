Writing Career

Nina has been writing short fiction books from a young age. After she met Kelly Wilson and helped train Shyla The Snowy Brumby she was inspired to record this amazing experinece. Kelly Wilson kindly offered to mentor her as well as edit the book that she started writing at age 11. At 16 she signed a contract with publisher Penguin Random House, and at 17 her dreams of being a published author will come true.
